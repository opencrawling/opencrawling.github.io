Meet the Core Architects

The steering team behind the Open Ingestion Standard (OIS) and OpenCrawling, building high-throughput, secure connectors bridging content to AI vector store architectures.

Piergiorgio Lucidi

Founder & Lead Architect

Piergiorgio is an Enterprise Integration Architect, Open Source advocate, and speaker. Having built large-scale content management and security engines for major European institutions, he founded OpenCrawling to solve the complex challenge of mapping enterprise security access controls (ACLs) to AI vector models.

Enterprise Security & ACL Mapping 95%
Java 25 & Spring Boot Core 90%
Enterprise Integration Patterns 85%
  • ⚡ OIS Core Specification schema design & stabilization.
  • ⚡ Java 25 Virtual Threads execution pool coordinator.
  • ⚡ Principal security mapper & Authority Translation service interfaces.

Michael Cizmar

Lead Architect

Michael is a senior systems architect specializing in high-volume, event-driven pipelines, big data infrastructures, and information retrieval architectures. Over his career, he has optimized enterprise search capabilities for corporations and spearheaded decentralized Kafka-based microservice clustering.

Event-Driven Pipelines (Kafka, Redis) 95%

Decoupled AI Vector Store Services 85%
